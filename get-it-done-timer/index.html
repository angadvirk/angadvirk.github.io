HELP


  • Pick a focus period, break duration, and number of cycles (the default of 25-minute focus periods with 5-minute breaks is recommended to start).
  • Work as fast and as efficiently as possible during the focus period.
  • Stop working, and relax during the break. Take deep breaths, meditate, take a walk, etc. Such activities will get your mind ready to focus once again.
  • NOTE: Don't start watching Netflix or browsing social media during your breaks. These activities can actually stress you out further. Instead, consciously relax.
  • Also, don't skip the breaks. I myself have a bad habit of sometimes skipping breaks because I want to finish working early, but I end up being less efficient because my mind gets tired.
  • Repeat this for as many cycles as you need to.
  • Practice daily to improve your focus.
  • Profit. Lots and lots of profit.
